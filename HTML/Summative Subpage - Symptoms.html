Preventions
There is no vaccine to prevent coronavirus infections. Daily preventative steps to take include avoiding affected regions, avoiding local hospitals or clinics unless necessary, avoiding eating uncooked meat or eggs, avoiding being in contact with animals and dead animals, and maintain good health habits.
